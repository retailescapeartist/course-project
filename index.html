A Few of My Favorite Things

What is your favorite? I usually don't answer these questions because it is hard to pick from things. I'll do my best in here to list some of my preferred games, movies, and music. When I set out to make this list, I had to do a lot of thinking. I've played lots of games, watched lots of movies, and listened to lots of music.

For games, I will be going from past titles to current titles. I wanted to include some current selections that I've been playing recently, along with some classics that I remember when I was younger. I haven't seen a great deal of newer movies, so those might not appear on my list. This category will feature older titles. For me, music is an interesting one. I like all sorts of genres, so it is hard for me to pick a few favorites. This list will be like the gaming list, mixed with old and new. I've decided to keep each list to my top 10 favorites, and why they are my favorites.
